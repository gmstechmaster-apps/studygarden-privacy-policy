StudyGarden Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

StudyGarden stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with third parties. Your habit data, focus sessions, and progress are stored only on your device.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally using SQLite on your device. If you enable the Backup & Restore feature (Premium), StudyGarden creates a backup file stored in the app's private storage on your device. On Android, this file is eligible for the device's standard Android Backup Service, which — if enabled in your phone's settings — securely stores it in your own Google Account; we do not operate our own cloud servers and never have access to this data ourselves. On iOS, the backup file currently stays local to your device only. Uninstalling the app will delete the local copy; an Android Backup copy, if one exists, follows your device's own backup settings.

3. Analytics

StudyGarden does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. No usage data is sent to external servers.

4. Advertising (Free Version)

The free version may show non-personalised ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers in accordance with Google's privacy policy. You can remove ads by upgrading to Premium.

5. In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not store payment information. Purchase signatures are checked on-device to help verify authenticity and prevent fraud.

6. Children's Privacy

StudyGarden is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@studygarden.app
